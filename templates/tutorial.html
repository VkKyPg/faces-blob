What it's About

Faces is a way to help you with associations! If you've ever met a bunch of people at once, you might need assistance remembering names. Just create a new category and add your new friends so you can refer back to them later!

You can even use Faces to help you remember other things like story book characters! If you've ever read a book and had trouble remembering how the characters relate, just add them to your book's category and write a short description to help you out.

Using Categories

Click the "Add Category" button at the top-right to add a category.

Click on the "delete" button inside the selected category tile in order to delete the category.

Adding a Person to a Category

Click on the "Add Person" button in order to add someone to the selected cateogry.

To insert an image URL, find an image on the internet, right click, and select "copy image URL". You can enter this URL to give your person an image to associate.

Click on the "delete" button insdie the selected person tile in order to delete a person from the category.
